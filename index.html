Love isn’t loud or rushed.
It’s a gift — placed gently in our lives by God,
meant to be handled with care.

It shows up in patience,
in presence,
in choosing someone even on ordinary days.

This isn’t about pressure or perfection.
It’s about intention.
You matter to me, and I wanted you to feel that
clearly, gently, honestly.

1 Corinthians 13:7
Love bears all things, believes all things,
hopes all things, endures all things.
